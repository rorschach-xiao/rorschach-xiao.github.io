## Short Bio
Welcome to my personal webpage! My name is **Yang Xiao** (Chinese: ËÇñÈò≥). I am currently a **Researcher** at **TikTok**. Prior to that, I earned my Master's degree in Computer Science from **Northeastern University**, where I was supervised by **Ryan Rad**. I also hold a Bachelor's degree from **Beihang University**.
