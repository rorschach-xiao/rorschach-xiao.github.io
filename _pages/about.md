# Short Bio
Welcome to my personal webpage! My name is **Yang Xiao** (Chinese: ËÇñÈò≥) and I'm currently a Master's candidate of Computer Science at **Northeastern University** (Vancouver, Canada) and also a intern at **TikTok**.
